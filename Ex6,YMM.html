You can find the list of Suggested Gardening Tools and Suplies

Gardens and gardeners come in all sizes, shapes, and needs. We may be short or tall, left-handed or right-handed. We may be starting a few planter boxes on the deck or growing entire rows of vegetable in the backyard. Thus, the tools that work for one person may ot work for another.

Complicating matters evenmore are tools that can serve multiple purposes. For example, some people might like a hand hoe for cleaning weeds while others use it for creating furrows for bulbs. A weeder is made for pulling up taproots but its narrow head also makes it ideal for dibbling holes for seeds. What can be agreed onts that having the right tools for your particular needs is essential for your sanity and success in the garden.

Use this shoppinglist as a uide of what might be useful when you hit the ground running. It's best to "try on" tools in person so you can get a feel for what's comfortable in your hand for propotionate to your height.
